Old Testament Characters

Creation and Early World

Adam

The first man created by God, husband of Eve.

Scripture References:

  • Creation: Genesis 1:26-27, 2:7
  • Fall: Genesis 3
  • Genealogy: Genesis 5:1-5

Eve

The first woman, created from Adam's rib, mother of all living.

Scripture References:

  • Creation: Genesis 2:21-25
  • Fall: Genesis 3
  • Children: Genesis 4:1-2, 25

Cain

First son of Adam and Eve, first murderer who killed his brother Abel.

Scripture References:

  • Birth: Genesis 4:1
  • Murder of Abel: Genesis 4:8
  • Punishment: Genesis 4:11-16

Abel

Second son of Adam and Eve, murdered by his brother Cain.

Scripture References:

  • Birth: Genesis 4:2
  • Offering: Genesis 4:4
  • Murder: Genesis 4:8

Seth

Third son of Adam and Eve, born after Abel's death.

Scripture References:

  • Birth: Genesis 4:25-26
  • Genealogy: Genesis 5:3-8

Enoch

Walked with God and was taken directly to heaven without dying.

Scripture References:

  • Life: Genesis 5:21-24
  • Faith: Hebrews 11:5

Methuselah

Longest-lived person recorded in the Bible (969 years).

Scripture References:

  • Life: Genesis 5:21-27

Lamech

Father of Noah, prophesied about his son's role.

Scripture References:

  • Life: Genesis 5:28-31
  • Prophecy: Genesis 5:29

The Flood and Post-Flood Era

Noah

Built the ark by God's command, saved humanity and animals from the flood.

Scripture References:

  • Called by God: Genesis 6:8-22
  • Flood Story: Genesis 7-8
  • God's Covenant: Genesis 9:1-17

Noah's Wife

Though unnamed in Scripture, she survived the flood with Noah.

Scripture References:

  • Mentioned: Genesis 6:18, 7:7, 8:16

Shem

Noah's eldest son, ancestor of Semitic peoples including the Hebrews.

Scripture References:

  • Genealogy: Genesis 10:21-31
  • Blessing: Genesis 9:26-27

Ham

Noah's son who saw his father's nakedness, father of Canaan.

Scripture References:

  • Sin against Noah: Genesis 9:20-25
  • Descendants: Genesis 10:6-20

Japheth

Noah's son whose descendants spread to coastal lands.

Scripture References:

  • Blessing: Genesis 9:27
  • Descendants: Genesis 10:2-5

Nimrod

Mighty hunter and builder of ancient cities including Babel.

Scripture References:

  • Description: Genesis 10:8-12

Canaan

Son of Ham who was cursed by Noah.

Scripture References:

  • Curse: Genesis 9:25-27
  • Descendants: Genesis 10:15-19

The Patriarchs

Abraham (Abram)

Father of the faithful, called by God to leave Ur and start a new nation.

Scripture References:

  • Call: Genesis 12:1-3
  • Covenant: Genesis 15, 17
  • Faith Test: Genesis 22

Sarah (Sarai)

Abraham's wife, mother of Isaac, known for her beauty and eventual faith.

Scripture References:

  • Beauty: Genesis 12:10-20
  • Promise: Genesis 18:1-15
  • Isaac's Birth: Genesis 21:1-7

Hagar

Sarah's Egyptian maidservant, mother of Ishmael.

Scripture References:

  • Given to Abraham: Genesis 16:1-4
  • Flight: Genesis 16:6-14
  • Sent Away: Genesis 21:9-21

Lot

Abraham's nephew who chose to live in Sodom.

Scripture References:

  • Separation: Genesis 13:5-13
  • Rescue: Genesis 14:12-16
  • Sodom: Genesis 19

Ishmael

Abraham's first son through Hagar, became father of Arab peoples.

Scripture References:

  • Birth: Genesis 16:15-16
  • Prophecy: Genesis 16:11-12
  • Sent Away: Genesis 21:8-21

Isaac

Son of promise born to Abraham and Sarah in their old age.

Scripture References:

  • Birth Promise: Genesis 17:19-21
  • Birth: Genesis 21:1-7
  • Sacrifice Story: Genesis 22

Rebekah

Wife of Isaac, mother of Jacob and Esau.

Scripture References:

  • Marriage: Genesis 24
  • Twins: Genesis 25:21-26
  • Deception: Genesis 27

Esau

Firstborn son of Isaac who sold his birthright for soup.

Scripture References:

  • Birth: Genesis 25:25
  • Birthright: Genesis 25:29-34
  • Blessing Lost: Genesis 27

Jacob (Israel)

Son of Isaac who became father of the twelve tribes of Israel.

Scripture References:

  • Birth: Genesis 25:26
  • Deception: Genesis 27
  • Wrestling: Genesis 32:22-32
